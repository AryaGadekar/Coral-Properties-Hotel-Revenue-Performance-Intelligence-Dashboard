## TL;DR

Built a **Power BI Hotel Revenue & Performance Intelligence Dashboard** using **164,314 daily room records** to evaluate portfolio performance, booking channel efficiency, and demand patterns across a multi-city hotel group.

The goal of this analysis was to move beyond basic reporting and identify **operational drivers of revenue, occupancy, and pricing performance** to support data-driven revenue management decisions.

**Key Findings**

- **Business travelers dominate demand**, contributing approximately **77% of total portfolio revenue**.
- **Weekend performance significantly outperforms weekdays**, with higher occupancy and ADR levels.
- **Coral Delhi emerges as the strongest-performing property**, consistently leading the portfolio in RevPAR and occupancy efficiency.
- **Booking platforms vary significantly in ADR and realization rates**, highlighting the need for channel mix optimization.
- **Unused room capacity represents a major revenue opportunity**, indicating growth potential without expanding inventory.

**Strategic Direction**

- Improve **weekday demand generation** to increase overall occupancy.
- Optimize **booking channel strategy** to balance booking volume and revenue realization.
- Replicate **top-performing property practices** across underperforming locations.
- Focus on **capacity utilization improvements** before expanding physical inventory.
